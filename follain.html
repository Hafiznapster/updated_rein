Project Overview

Commercial Solar Installation Excellence

Over a dedicated three-week installation period, a state-of-the-art 484 kWp solar photovoltaic (PV) system was successfully commissioned at Follain's facility in Cork, Ireland.

This project represents a major step forward in the company's ongoing sustainability journey and commitment to reducing environmental impact through clean, renewable energy.

Key Features

Project Highlights

The Follain Solar Project incorporates several innovative features that make it a standout commercial renewable energy installation.

High-Efficiency Panels

590W JA Solar panels providing maximum energy generation in limited roof space with superior performance.

Exceptional Output

81+ MWh of clean electricity generated, offsetting 20% of the facility's grid consumption.

Environmental Impact

40 tons of CO₂ emissions prevented, equivalent to planting approximately 53 mature trees.

Advanced Technology

Three Huawei inverters delivering 330 kW of AC capacity with exceptional reliability and efficiency.
