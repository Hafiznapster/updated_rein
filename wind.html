Wind Turbine Types

We offer various types of wind turbines to suit different applications and site conditions.

Residential Turbines

Small-scale wind turbines designed for residential properties and small businesses.

Capacity: 1-10 kW
Height: 10-30 meters
Application: Homes, farms

Commercial Turbines

Medium-scale wind turbines for commercial and industrial applications.

Capacity: 100 kW - 1 MW
Height: 50-80 meters
Application: Businesses, industries

Utility-Scale Turbines

Large-scale wind turbines for utility and commercial wind farm projects.

Capacity: 1.5-3 MW+
Height: 80-120 meters
Application: Wind farms, utilities
